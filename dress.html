Singapore's Traditional Dress/Clothes

Baju Kurung

A traditional Malay outfit worn by both men and women. For women, it’s a long blouse paired with a matching skirt; for men, it’s a long-sleeved shirt with trousers and a sarong. Commonly worn during festivals like Hari Raya.

Cheongsam (Qipao)

A traditional Chinese dress for women, known for its high collar, tight fit, and side slits. It’s elegant and often made from silk with floral embroidery — usually worn during Chinese New Year or weddings.

Sari

A traditional Indian garment made of a long piece of cloth (about 5–6 meters) wrapped around the body with one end draped over the shoulder. It’s colorful and often decorated with intricate patterns, symbolizing grace and beauty.
