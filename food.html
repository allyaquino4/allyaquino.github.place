6 Famous Foods in Singapore

Hainanese Chicken Rice

Steamed or roasted chicken served with fragrant rice cooked in chicken broth. Often called Singapore’s national dish.

Chilli Crab

Juicy crab cooked in a sweet, spicy, and tangy tomato-based sauce. Best eaten with mantou (fried buns).

Laksa

A spicy noodle soup made with coconut milk, shrimp, fish cake, and bean sprouts — rich and flavorful.

Char Kway Teow

Stir-fried flat rice noodles with soy sauce, egg, shrimp, and Chinese sausage. Smoky and savory.

Satay

Skewered and grilled meat (usually chicken or beef) served with a rich peanut sauce and cucumber slices.

Hokkien Mee

Stir-fried yellow noodles and rice noodles in prawn and squid broth, often topped with lime and sambal.
